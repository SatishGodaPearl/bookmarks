**GWBrowser is a simple digital asset manager aimed at small and medium-sized
animation and film productions.**

It helps you navigate digital productions by creating and annotating the files
and folders used to keep projects. To learn more on what GWBrowser does and how you
can have a look at the :ref:`overview` or :ref:`modules-documentation`.


.. image:: _static/banner.png
   :alt: GWBrowser



Get GWBrowser
=============

You can get the latest version from `Github Releases <https://github.com/wgergely/GWBrowser/releases/>`_
(see the notes about building from source: :ref:`installing-from-source`).
